An application fee of $40 is required when applying to the nursery school. After this is received, your child is placed on an enrollment list. You will be notified of acceptance in the March preceding the fall enrollment date. Upon acceptance, a nonrefundable deposit of one month’s tuition is due. This will be applied to tuition for the following May.

You may fill out an Application for Admission online. You may also print out and return the pdf version: Application for Admission WUNS

You must fill out the online Enrollment Form every year that your child attends WUNS.

Before Their First Day:

Once accepted into the school, the following fill out forms must be filled out and return. This is done only once.
- Developmental History Online Form
- Child Medical Examination Report Form
- Immunization Consent/History Form

Orientation:

Orientation is usually the day after Labor Day and gives the child and parent(s) an opportunity to visit the classroom and meet the teachers and classmates. This visit is one hour. The next day, a child will attend school on his/her own (either Wednesday or Thursday, see orientation schedule). The class is divided in half, attending on either Wednesday or Thursday. This schedule allows the children and teachers to interact with a small group before the entire class attends on Friday.


Parents interested in Washington University Nursery School should contact the school to schedule a visit.
